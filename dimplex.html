What These Numbers Mean

Total Energy Register: The cumulative lifetime energy consumed by the heater. This never resets unless power is lost for a long period or the PCB is replaced.

Yesterday Energy Register: The value recorded at the end of the previous day. Subtracting this from the current total gives an estimate of daily consumption.

Charge Window Duration: How long the heater is allowed to charge (typically during off-peak hours). This affects the effective charging power.

Why this matters: These heaters don’t show historical usage clearly. This page reconstructs daily kWh and cost so you can understand real behaviour rather than guess.
